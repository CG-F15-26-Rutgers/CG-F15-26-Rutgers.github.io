Part 4

Breakdown
For part 4, we had to implement an increase to the weight of the hueristic cost. Essentially, to create weighted A*. We considered the bigger g-value for setting the tie-breaker between f-values. However, the number of expanded nodes and length of the path do not change when the heuristic is increased.
