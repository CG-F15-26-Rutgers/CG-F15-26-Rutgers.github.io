Assignment A5

Crowd Simulation Challenge

Description

Breakdown
This is the culmination project where we put together everything we learned this semester to create our own Crowd Simulator. Click here to go to our Github.

There were 11 different testcases:

  • Plane Egress
  • Plane Ingress
  • Crowd Crossing
  • Office Complex
  • Hallway Four Way Rounded Roundabout
  • Bottleneck Squeeze
  • Doorway Two Way
  • Double Squeeze
  • Wall Squeeze
  • Hallway Two Way
  • Maze

We modified the Social Forces AI to be adaptable to each of these testcases.
