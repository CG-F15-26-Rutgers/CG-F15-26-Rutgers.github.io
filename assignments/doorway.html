Assignment A5

Crowd Simulation Challenge

Description

Doorway Two Way

Breakdown
In this testcase, two agents try to get through a single doorway. We wanted to avoid them colliding so we had the bottom agent go through the door first. The top agent travels upwards just a little bit and then goes around the second agent and through the door and to its target.

Again, we utilized the _goalQueue for rearranging the goals.
