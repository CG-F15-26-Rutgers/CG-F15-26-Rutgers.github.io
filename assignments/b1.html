Assignment B1

Navigation and Animation

Description

Part 1: Agent Navigation
Our implementation allows users to click a destination before clicking on agents. You may click multiple agents to go towards a destination

Part 2: Mecanim Animation
Here users get to test the animation of a simple rig provided by the Raw Mocap package. Users may use (w) to move forward and (s) to move backward as well as (space) to jump.

Part 3: Animation and Navigation
We removed the capsule agents for the rig used in Part 2. The functionality is the same as Part 1. Users click on a destination then agent. You may also click multiple agents at a time.

Bonus Features
We added one Nazgul agent that repels agents that go toward it. You may move the Nazgul agent the same way as other agents.

We added eight objects that move automatically within two scenes. Four tall cylinders, and four little cubes.

We also added a menu to navigate between the three scenes at the start of the playable demo.
